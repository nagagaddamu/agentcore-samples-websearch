# Amazon Bedrock Managed Knowledge Base

> [!NOTE]
> This workshop lab is coming soon.

![arch](../images/architecture.png)

Attach Amazon Bedrock Managed Knowledge Base to your gateway as a built-in connector so agents can retrieve from your organizational knowledge through a standard MCP tool. You connect your unstructured data sources and AgentCore manages the vector store, the embedding and re-ranking models, and scalability concerns, so there is no ingestion pipeline to build or retrieval to tune. Its agentic retriever plans queries across your knowledge bases, connects related concepts across documents, evaluates intermediate results, and re-ranks before answering.
